Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery areas?

We currently deliver to all major areas within the city and some surrounding suburbs. Enter your pincode on our website to check if we deliver to your area.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive a tracking link via SMS and email. You can also track your order in real-time through our mobile app or website.

What is your return policy?

We have a hassle-free return policy. If you're not satisfied with any product, you can return it within 7 days of delivery for a full refund or replacement.

How can I become a vendor?

We're always looking for quality vendors to partner with. Please fill out the vendor application form on our website or contact our business development team.
